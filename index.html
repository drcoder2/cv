Dan Ricci

Senior Cybersecurity Professional and Innovator for IT and OT network environments

Dan is Senior ICS Cybersecurity Engineer and innovator focused in control systems and Internet of Things (IoT) environments. He retired from the U.S. Navy after serving 21 years in the Information Warfare community. During his career he supported and enabled full spectrum Cyberspace operations, Naval Air, Sea, and Special Warfare operations. He further possesses skills in managing and performing Cyber network defense analysis, operations planning, program management, Cybersecurity governance, information assurance management, vulnerability management processes for IT and OT/ICS environments. Managed research and development projects for DOD Cyberspace capability development and acquisition.
